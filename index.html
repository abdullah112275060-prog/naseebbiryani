Our Story

Karachi Ki Legendary Biryani

Since 1999, Naseeb Biryani & Pakwan Center has been serving the authentic, heartwarming taste of Karachi's most beloved biryani. Born in the vibrant streets of Mehmoodabad, our recipes carry the soul of traditional Pakistani cooking — slow-cooked with hand-picked spices, fragrant basmati rice, and the love that every great desi khana deserves.

From humble beginnings to becoming a household name in Karachi, we have never compromised on taste, quality, or authenticity. Every plate tells the story of Pakistan's rich culinary heritage.
